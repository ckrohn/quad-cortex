== Purpose

This is the warmer, less chorused clean guitar. Path B is active and Path A is muted at the Mixer.

[source,text]
----
Path B:
VCA Comp -> US DLX 65 Reissue -> 112 US DLX SC64

Post-mixer:
Low-High Cut -> Parametric-8 -> Chief CE2W (M) -> Digital Delay (M) -> Plate -> Gain -> Output
----

== Clean correction table

[%autowidth,cols="1,1",options="header"]
|===
|Problem |Change
|Clean is boomy |Raise Low Cut to 130 Hz and lower Bass to 3.0
|Clean is too bright |Lower High Cut to 8.0 kHz and lower Treble to 4.5
|Clean clips with Fishmans |Lower Volume to 2.3 to 2.6
|Clean is too dry compared with the other guitar |Raise Chief CE2W Mix to 18%
|Delay/reverb is too wet |Reduce Delay Mix to 8% and Plate Mix to 10%
|===
